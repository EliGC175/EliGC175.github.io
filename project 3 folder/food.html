Food

There are many different types of foods in minecraft, each food has a different saturation. So depending on the food you eat your hunger will increase different amounts. Some foods give you negative or positive affect depending on the food you eat for example if you eat raw flesh you'll get hunger effect that can last up to 30 seconds

There are 40 different types of food you can consume in Minecraft, some include...

  • Chicken
  • Apples
  • Wheat
  • Carrots
  • Beef
  • Potatoes
